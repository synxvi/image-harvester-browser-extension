Rule Examples

  • Match: ^(https?://pbs\.twimg\.com/media/[^?]+)\?format=(\w+)&name=.*$
    Replace: $1.$2:orig — Twitter/X thumbnail to original
  • Match: ^(https?://preview\.redd\.it/[^?]+)\?.*$
    Replace: $1 — Reddit remove query parameters
  • Match: ^https?://i\.imgur\.com/(\w+)([tsmlbh])?\.(\w+)$
    Replace: https://i.imgur.com/$1.$3 — Imgur remove thumbnail suffix
